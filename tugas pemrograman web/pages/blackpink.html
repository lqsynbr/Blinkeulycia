BLACKPINK

On June 29, 2016, YG Entertainment revealed the final line-up and name of the group. BLACKPINK is the first girl group to debut under YG Entertainment since 2NE1's debut in 2009. The group debuted on August 8 with their first digital single album, "Square One". The single album has two songs, "Whistle" and "Boombayah" which are the title tracks for the album. "Whistle" was composed by Teddy Park, Future Bounce, and Bekuh BOOM and written by Teddy Park and Bekuh BOOM. "Boombayah" was composed and written by Teddy Park and Bekuh BOOM.[5] Beomjin J directed the music video for "Whistle" while Seo Hyun-seung did for "Boombayah." "Boombayah" debuted at #1 and "Whistle" debuted at #2 on the Billboard World Digital Songs Chart. They are the third Korean artist to hold the top spots, alongside PSY and Big Bang. "Whistle" topped the digital, download, and streaming monthly chart of Gaon Chart for August 2016. "Whistle" also reached #1 on the weekly, popularity, music video, and K-pop music video chart of China’s biggest music-streaming website QQ Music. BLACKPINK made their first music show performance and performed "Whistle" and "Boombayah" for the first time at Inkigayo on August 14. The group took home their first music show win for their song "Whistle" on August 21 at Inkigayo. It took them 14 days to achieve their first win and are the fourth fastest girl group to win their first. They wrapped up their promotions for "Square One" on September 11 at Inkigayo and scored another win for "Whistle". BLACKPINK released their second digital single album, "Square Two" on November 1 with "Playing With Fire" and "Stay" serving as the title tracks. Both songs were produced by Teddy, R.Tee, and Seo Won Jin. "Playing With Fire" debuted at #1 on the Billboard World Digital Songs chart, making it their second song to do so. The group had their comeback stage at Inkigayo on August 6 and M Countdown on August 10 where they performed "Playing With Fire" and "Stay".
